About North Social

The Innovator Of Record Since 2006

North Social has been building innovative and impactful social media applications, promotions, and marketing campaigns for some of the most forward thinking brands on the planet since 2006

In February 2011, North Social was acquired by Vocus Corporation, a Maryland based provider of marketing and PR software. (VOCS) You can read more about Vocus at www.vocus.com

Alex Bernstein

Business Strategy & Planning

Alex fuels, drives, and steers platform strategy, operations, and business development for North Social (clicks, conversions, and viral drivers are his world). Prior to co-founding North in 2006, Bernstein was a Senior Vice President at Virgin Entertainment Group where he led strategy, operations, and corporate development. Living up to the title of serial entrepreneur, two years before iTunes launched Alex co-founded and served as Executive Vice President for a digital content distribution platform called Echo, which was both politically backed and funded by leading media retailers: Best Buy, Virgin, Borders, Tower Records, TransWorld Entertainment, Hastings, and Wherehouse. Prior to entering the business world Alex spent four seasons in the NFL as an offensive lineman with the Baltimore Ravens, New York Jets, Cleveland Browns, and Atlanta Falcons.
